## Ingredients

- 1-½ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 Tbsp white sugar
- 0.875 tsp baking soda
- 2 Tbsp fiber powder
- 1.75 tsp vinegar
- 1-¼ cups milk
- 1 egg
- 2 Tbsp butter, melted

## Instructions

1. Mix dry ingredients, then wet ingredients, then pour into a measuring glass for easy pouring.
