## Ingredients

- Frozen broccoli florets
- Frozen cauliflower florets
- Olive oil (a couple of tablespoons)
- Salt
- Pepper
- Onion powder
- Garlic powder

## Instructions

- Set the oven to 425, and preheat with the sheet pan IN the oven.
- Cut any of the frozen florets into bite-sized chunks (slightly larger is okay) with a sharp butcher knife.  Be VERY careful!
- While the oven is heating, in a bowl, mix all ingredients on the frozen florets.
- When oven reaches temperature, take out the sheet pan.
- Spread out the florets as much as you can, leaving space between them.  We don't want them to be too close together.  If they are, they will steam instead of bake.
- Put the pan in the oven and set the timer for 30 minutes.
- After 15 minutes, take out the pan and turn each of the florets.
- Place back in the oven for the remaining 15 minutes.
- Optional:  If you want some extra 'charring' on the florets, you can raise the temperature to 450 for the last 5 or 10 minutes, but we often don't find this necessary.
