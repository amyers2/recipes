## Ingredients

- 1 lb strawberries
- 1/6 cup chia seeds
- 1 Tbsp maple syrup / honey

## Instructions

1. Quarter the strawberries and add to large pan.
2. Cook over medium heat until soft and bubbling.
3. Remove from heat and use a potato masher to gently mash the strawberries to the desired consistency.
4. Add chia seeds and sweetener.
5. Set aside to cool slightly, then place into jar.
6. Jam will completely set once refrigerated for at least 4 hours.
