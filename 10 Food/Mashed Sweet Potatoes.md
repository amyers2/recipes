## Ingredients

- 5(?) Sweet Potatoes
- Butter
- Brown Sugar
- Cinnamon Plus (Pampered Chef spice blend)
- Salt (dash)

## Directions

1. Peel sweet potatoes.
2. Cut potatoes into pieces.
3. Put potatoes in bowl, along with all ingredients.
4. Microwave until mashable (8 min?).
5. Mash them.
