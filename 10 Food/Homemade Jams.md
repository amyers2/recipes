## Ingredients

- 1 lb fruit of choice
- 1/6 cup chia seeds
- 1 Tbsp maple syrup / honey (if desired)

## Instructions

1. Optional: cut up / thaw fruit to make cooking easier.
	- Note: Fruit that is easier by thawing first: Strawberries, Blackberries, Cherries
2. Add fruit to large pan.
3. Cook over medium heat until soft and bubbling.
4. Remove from heat and use a potato masher to gently mash to the desired consistency.
5. Add chia seeds and sweetener.
	- Note: Less than full amount of chia seeds if fruit is less than 1 lb.
6. Set aside to cool slightly, then place into jar.
7. Jam will completely set once refrigerated for at least 4 hours.

## Notes

Successful jams we've done this way:
- Strawberry
- Raspberry
- Blueberry
- Blackberry
- Dark Sweet Cherry
