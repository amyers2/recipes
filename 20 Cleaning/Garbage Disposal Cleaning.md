## Ingredients

- Cool water
- Squirt of dish washing soap
- 1 cup vinegar
- Drain plug

## Directions

1. Plug garbage disposal. 
2. Fill sink ¾ of the way with cool water. 
3. Add squirt of dish washing soap. 
4. Add 1 cup of vinegar. 
5. Mix it all good. 
6. Turn on cold water, remove the plug, and turn on the disposal.
